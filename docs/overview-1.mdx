We power transactions across:

* **16+ inbound currencies**
* **26+ outbound currencies**
* **Settlement in USDC and USDT on multiple networks**

With LINK, you can:

* On-ramp local currency to USDC/USDT instantly
* Off-ramp USDC/USDT to local currency instantly
* Send cross-border in 256+ currency pairs (API coming soon; OTC available now)
* Pre-fund FX flows via the LINK Business Dashboard to automate B2B payouts

### What you can build with LINK?

* Local currency **on- and off-ramp rails** to USDC & USDT in 26 currencies
* **Send & receive payments** - Move funds globally using fiat rails, stablecoins, or supported digital assets
* **Virtual accounts** - Instantly issue USD, EUR, NGN, and 13 deposit accounts for your users with local bank details
