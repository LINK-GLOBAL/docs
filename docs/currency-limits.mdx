### For example:

* Customer sends: 35,000.00 BRL.
* Amount delivered: 6,435.66 USDC

## Transaction Limits

Currency limits apply when onramping or offramping to **USDC** or **USDT**, and may vary depending on the currency and payment method used. This is to cover associated blockchain or liquidity costs.
