### 🔁 Ramp Orchestration: Access Digital Dollars

**LINK Bridge Ramp orchestration APIs** allows you power fiat-to-digital assets and vice versa for both your **retail, business,** and **enterprise** customers.

| API type | Built for | You are a | Key features |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Retail API** | Consumer-facing apps & wallets | A fintech, wallet, or payments app serving individual users | \* Instant on/off-ramps for 6\+ currencies<br />\* Multi-chain Static Account |
| B2B API | Platforms & enterprises | A SaaS platform, exchange, or fintech serving business clients | \* On/off-ramps for 16\+ pay-in and 26\+ payout currencies<br />\* Multiple processing modes (Default or Pre-funded) |

---

#### Additional Capabilities:

- **Virtual Accounts (Coming Soon)** — Unique fiat deposit addresses (ACH, Wire, SEPA, SPEI, and more) in 16\+ currencies for end-customers. Ideal for recurring deposits. _(B2B only)_
- **Multi-chain Static Templates** — Reusable fiat payment transfer instructions for on/off-ramps across all supported networks. _(Retail & B2B)_
- **Static Specific Templates** — Reusable fiat transfer instructions with custom references & liquidation addresses on a specific network. _(B2B only)_
- **Liquidation Addresses** — On-chain addresses that automatically forward stablecoins to a fixed destination or your LINK Business wallet. _(B2B only)_
- **Prefunded Accounts**— Preload fiat (26\+ currencies) that auto-converts to USDC into your LINK Business Account Wallet for instant on/off-ramps without settlement delays. _(B2B only)_

---

### 💱 Cross border FX Orchestration: Move Money across borders

1. **APIs (Coming Soon)** — Programmatically move between fiat-to-fiat currency pairs. _(B2B only)_
2. **Platform & OTC** — Open a LINK Business Account to move funds across **670\+ currency pairs** for treasury management, prefunded flows, and more. _(B2B only)_

### 👤 Customers: Onboarding & compliance

LINK Bridge APIs include built-in tools for user onboarding and compliance:

- **Retail API** — Full KYC handled automatically by LINK.
- **B2B API** — KYB is integrated and can also be performed manually if needed.
