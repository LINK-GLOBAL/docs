---

### 🔁 Ramp Orchestration: Access Digital Dollars

**LINK Bridge Ramp Orchestration APIs** allow you to power fiat-to-digital assets and vice versa for both your **retail, business,** and **enterprise** customers.

| API type | Built for | Who you are | What you get |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [**Retail API**](/docs/retail-service) | Consumer-facing apps & wallets | A fintech, wallet, or payments app serving individual users | Inbound and outbound payments for multiple currencies \* Multi-chain Static Account |
| [**Business API**](/docs/otc-service) | Platforms & enterprises | An exchange, fintech, OTC desk, or liquidity provider serving businesses and institutional clients | Multiple inbound and outbound payment currencies across 25+ currencies |

---

#### Additional Capabilities:

- **Virtual Accounts (Coming Soon)** — Unique fiat deposit addresses (ACH, Wire, SEPA, SPEI, and more) in multiple currencies for end-customers. Ideal for recurring deposits. _(Business API only)_
- **Multi-chain Static Templates** — Reusable fiat payment transfer instructions for on/off-ramps across all supported networks. _(Retail & Business API)_
- **Static Specific Templates** — Reusable fiat transfer instructions with custom references & liquidation addresses on a specific network. _(Business API only)_
- **Liquidation Addresses** _(Coming Soon)_ — On-chain addresses that automatically forward stablecoins to a fixed destination or your LINK Business wallet. _(Business API only)_
- **Prefunded Accounts** _(Coming Soon)_ — Preload fiat that auto-converts to USDC into your LINK Business Account Wallet for instant on/off-ramps without settlement delays. _(Business API only)_

---

### 💱 Cross-Border FX Orchestration: Move Money Across Borders

1. **APIs (Coming Soon)** — Programmatically move between fiat-to-fiat currency pairs. _(Business API only)_
2. **Platform & OTC** — Open a LINK Business Account to move funds across **670+ currency pairs** for treasury management, prefunded flows, and more. _(Business API only)_

---

### 👤 Customers: Onboarding & Compliance

LINK Bridge APIs include built-in tools for user onboarding and compliance:

- **Retail API** — Full KYC handled automatically by LINK.
- **Business API** — KYB is integrated and can also be performed manually if needed.
