<Warning>
  **NOTE:** First-party and third-party functionality is currently available **only for withdrawals**.
</Warning>

## First Party and Third Party Support

* **First Party:** The payment is made directly from or to the customer’s own account or wallet.
* **Third Party:** The payment is made from or to an external account or wallet that belongs to a third party, as authorized by the customer.

***

## First Party Support Only

The LINK B2B API supports first-party withdrawals only. Each business end-user created by a merchant is KYB-verified by LINK to enable compliant payment flows.
