### Integration Support?

If you have any questions about the integration of our exchange service, if you would like to report bugs or if you would like to request a feature, you can reach out to us on WhatsApp.

### User Support?

If end users of the service send you support requests:

* If they can't find what they're looking for there, ask them to write us at [linkio.operations@gmail.com](mailto:linkio.operations@gmail.com)

* For slow delays with payout or transaction errors, please reach out to us at [linkio.operations@gmail.com](mailto:linkio.operations@gmail.com) or send a message in the dedicated Telegram group we have with your business.

* Looking for us to support a new asset or network? [Contact us](mailto:linkio.operations@gmail.com) to become a partner.
