## Overview

**LINK Static Specific accounts** are **on-request, permanent, reusable fiat deposit addresses** with **randomly generated compulsory references.** These accounts automatically convert incoming fiat into **USDC** or **USDT** and deliver it to your specified destination (e.g., a wallet address) on only one of the supported blockchains. **[See supported blockchains](https://docs.linkio.world/v2.1/docs/stablecoins-and-blockchains#/)**

<Warning>
  ### **IMPORTANT:** The generated references are **compulsory for all transactions.** They ensure smooth processing and help resolve any disputes efficiently.
</Warning>

Effectively, they serve as an **on-ramp to liquidation addresses,** with your reference code acting as the **primary identifier.**

They support **multiple currencies** and provide customers with **local deposit details,** such as account numbers and routing codes.

For example, a **USD virtual account** gives your customer a **U.S. account and routing number** for **ACH** or **wire transfers.**

<Info>
  ### **NOTE:** This is supported **only by our B2B APIs.**
</Info>
