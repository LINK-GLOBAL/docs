### Key Capabilities:

* **Statics Acounts:** Receive fiat in **15+ currencies** via local payment methods
* **Personal Wallet Address:** Custodial, secure wallets supporting multiple networks, including **Base, Solana, Stellar,** and **Polygon**

Each supported network provides a unique wallet address tied to your business, enabling on-chain deposits and automated settlement.

***

### Usage

Use your Wallets to:

* Receive stablecoins from customers
* Move funds seamlessly between currencies
* Trigger payouts directly from your dashboard or via API

<Info>
  ### **IMPORTANT:** Each wallet address is unique to your business. Deposits sent to the wrong address or unsupported network may be permanently lost.
</Info>
