---
title: "Multichain Static Details"
description: "Multichain Static Details enable retail businesses (B2B2C) to provide their customers with permanent, reusable fiat deposit accounts."
---

<Warning>
  ### **IMPORTANT:** Each account includes a **randomized reference code** which are **compulsory for all transactions.** They ensure smooth processing and help resolve any disputes efficiently.
</Warning>

Deposits made into these accounts are **automatically converted to USDC or USDT** and delivered to the designated **wallet address** across LINK’s supported blockchain networks.

---

### Key Details

- Designed for **retail platforms** serving end-users (B2B2C).
- Supports deposits in NGN currency with **local banking details** (e.g., account numbers, routing codes).
- Supports **payouts to multiple blockchain networks.**
