Fiat sent to these accounts are **automatically converted into USDC or USDT** and **transferred to a whitelisted blockchain address** on one of our supported blockchain networks.

<Warning>
  ### **IMPORTANT:** The reference codes are **compulsory for all transactions.** Payments without a reference code **will not be processed**.
</Warning>

<Warning>
  ### **IMPORTANT:** Only whitelisted blockchain addresses can receive converted funds. Ensure all destination wallets are verified.
</Warning>

<Info>
  ### **NOTE:** Static Specific Accounts are only available for on-ramps.
</Info>

***

## Get static specific accounts

To retrieve a Static Specific Account, call the **static\_specific** endpoint.

You specify:

* The fiat currency for the account
* Configure the **wallet\_address** and **network** parameters to determine where the converted funds will be delivered.

LINK handles the fiat-to-stablecoin conversion and automatically sends the funds on-chain to the designated wallet.
