When a customer sends stablecoins (e.g., **USDC**) to their liquidation address, LINK will automatically transfers the funds to the configured destination.

***

### Choosing a Liquidation Address

When setting up a Liquidation Address, businesses can choose where incoming funds are delivered.

There are two options:

* **External blockchain address:** Route funds directly to a blockchain wallet that you control
* **LINK Business USD wallet:** Convert and settle funds into your **LINK Business USD wallet** for centralized treasury management.

<Info>
  ### **NOTE:** Once a Liquidation Address is linked to a destination, you'll need to contact \*\*

  [our support team](support@linkio.africa)\*\* to reassign a new one.
</Info>
