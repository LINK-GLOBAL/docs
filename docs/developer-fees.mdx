## Overview

LINK enables developers to define and collect **custom per-transaction fees** from their users. These fees are automatically deducted from the customer’s transaction amount and reserved for you.

**At the end of each business day**, all accumulated fees are **settled and disbursed in USDC** to the wallet address you've configured.

***

### Configuring Payout Address

Developers can choose where fee settlements are delivered:

* **Default Option:** Sent directly to the wallet associated with your LINK Business Account, using your **business ID**
* **Custom Option:** Sent to a blockchain wallet address that you specify
