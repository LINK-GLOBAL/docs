## Regional Availability

**LINK** can be used to facilitate payments for customers in **most countries** that are **not on the OFAC sanctions list.**

***

## International Deposit Details

Depending on your region, businesses should use **specific international deposit details:**

| Region            | International details to use                   |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Africa**        | International (SWIFT) details labeled **(EU)** |
| **Asia**          | International (SWIFT) details labeled **(SG)** |
| **Europe**        | International (SWIFT) details labeled **(EU)** |
| **Latin America** | International (SWIFT) details labeled **(EU)** |
| **North America** | International (SWIFT) details labeled **(EU)** |
| **Oceania**       | International (SWIFT) details labeled **(EU)** |

***

## Exclusions

* Businesses in **Asian countries (excluding Singapore):** Depositing **CNY** or **HKD** should use **International (SWIFT) details labeled (HK)**
* Businesses in **Southeast Asia:** Depositing **CNY** or **HKD** should use **International (SWIFT) details labeled (SG)**
