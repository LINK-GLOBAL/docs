### Business Verification

Before you can start using the **Retail API** or **B2B API**, your must complete **Know Your Business (KYB) verification**.

This process ensures that LINK can securely and compliantly process **stablecoin** or **fiat transactions** between your wallets and bank accounts.

<Info>
  ### **NOTE:** Each business is responsible for onboarding and verifying it's own end-users unless otherwise required by LINK's compliance policies.
</Info>

### Business ID Verification

To verify your business, use the business\_id found on your [LINK Dashboard](https://app.linkio.world).

Once verification is successful, your **API credentials** will automatically be applied to all subsequent operations.

### End-User Onboarding

In certain cases for example, when a user exceeds their daily transaction limit. LINK may be required to **directly onboard your end-user** to complete a transaction.

This process will still occur under your registered **business\_id** to maintain compliance continuity.
