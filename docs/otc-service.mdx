LINK's OTC desk handles high-value cross-border payments across **25 supported currencies**, covering major fiat markets in Africa, the Middle East, Europe, and beyond.

### Key Benefits of OTC Services:

* **Extended Currency Coverage:** Access to currencies beyond our standard API/dashboard offerings
* **Large Volume Handling:** Optimized for high-value transactions and institutional needs
* **Custom Payment Rails:** Specialized routing through regional banking networks
* **Dedicated Support:** Personal relationship management for complex cross-border payments
* **Competitive Rates:** Institutional-grade pricing for volume transactions

### Supported OTC Currencies:

| Payment Method        | Currencies                                                                          |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **ACH**               | USD                                                                                 |
| **Local Bank Transfer** | NGN, BIF, CDF, DZD, ETB, GBP, GHS, LYD, MUR, MWK, MZN, SGD, SLE, SSP, TZS, UGX |
| **IBAN**              | AED, AOA, EGP, EUR, TRY                                                             |
| **RIB**               | MAD, XAF, XOF                                                                       |

> **GBP** is supported under both Local Bank Transfer and IBAN depending on the account type.
