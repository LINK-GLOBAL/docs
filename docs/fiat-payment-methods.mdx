### LINK supports the following fiat currencies and rails.

| ISO | Currency                    | Supported Rails                                       |
| --- | --------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| AED | United Arab Emirates Dirham | IPI, RTGS, UAEFTS, and SWIFT                          |
| AUD | Australian Dollar           | Bank Transfer, BECS, NPP, BPAY, and SWIFT             |
| BRL | Brazilian Real              | Pix and Bank Transfer                                 |
| CAD | Canadian Dollar             | EFT, Interac, Bill Payments, and SWIFT                |
| CNY | Chinese Yuan                | ACH Transfer, RTGS Transfer. FPS Transfer and SWIFT   |
| DKK | Danish Krone                | Bank Transfer and SWIFT                               |
| EGP | Egyptian Pounds             | Bank Transfer and SWIFT                               |
| EUR | Euro                        | SEPA, SEPA Instant, TARGET2, and SWIFT                |
| GBP | British Pound               | Faster Payments, CHAPS, SEPA Instant, SEPA, and SWIFT |
| HKD | Hong Kong Dollar            | ACH, FPS, CHATS Transfer and SWIFT                    |
| IDR | Indonesian rupiah           | SKN / BI-FAST / iACH and SWIFT                        |
| ILS | Israeli Shekel              | MASAV, ZAHAV, and SWIFT                               |
| INR | Indian rupee                | Bank Transfer and SWIFT                               |
| KRW | South Korean won            | Bank Transfer and SWIFT                               |
| MXN | Mexican Peso                | SPEI and SWIFT                                        |
| MYR | Malaysian Ringgit           | Bank Transfer and SWIFT                               |
| NGN | Nigerian Naira              | Bank Transfer                                         |
| PHP | Philippine Peso             | Bank Transfer and E-wallet                            |
| SGD | Singapore Dollar            | FAST, GIRO, RTGS, and SWIFT                           |
| USD | US Dollar                   | ACH, Wire, and SWIFT                                  |
| ZAR | South African Rand          | SWIFT                                                 |
