## Overview

**LINK Multichain Static accounts** are **on-request, permanent, reusable fiat deposit addresses** with a **randomly generated reference.**

These accounts automatically convert incoming fiat into **USDC** or **USDT** and deliver it to your specified destination (e.g a wallet address) on the supported blockchains. [**See supported blockchains**](https://docs.linkio.world/v2.1/docs/stablecoins-and-blockchains#/)

<Warning>
  ### **IMPORTANT:** The generated references are **compulsory for all transactions.** They ensure smooth processing and help resolve any disputes efficiently.
</Warning>

They support **multiple currencies** and provide customers with **local deposit details,** such as account numbers and routing codes. [**See supported Payment Rails**](https://docs.linkio.world/v2.1/docs/fiat-payment-methods#/)

For example, a **USD virtual account** provides your customer a U.S. account and routing number for **ACH** or **wire transfers.**

---

Multichain Static accounts are available via the **Retail and B2B APIs** and can also be managed through the **LINK Business Dashboard (GUI).**

<Info>
  ### **IMPORTANT:** Customers must be **onboarded and KYC/KYB-approved** before using these accounts.
</Info>
