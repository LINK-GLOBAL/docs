You control how funds flow and where they go, while **LINK** manages the **regulatory, technical,** and **conversion** complexity behind the scenes.

---

### How It Works

1. **Setup paths** – Define the currency and payment type through which fiat enters, and specify where the resulting crypto is sent.
2. **Collect or designate funding** – LINK supports **prefunded accounts, local payment methods,** and **bank deposits.** You ensure sufficient funds are available to enable **instant on-ramps.**
3. **Initiate transactions** – When a customer requests an on-ramp, LINK’s **orchestration logic** automatically checks the funding source, applies fee rules, retrieves conversion rates, and verifies the destination wallet (either a **LINK Business Account** or a **liquidation address**).
4. **Execution & settlement** – LINK executes the transaction, performs the conversion, and sends the resulting funds to the destination wallet.
5. **Reconciliation & reporting** – Receive real-time status updates, confirmations, and complete visibility for auditing transaction flows.

---

### Compliance & Requirements

Support for geographies, currencies, and asset types can vary.

<Warning>

</Warning>
